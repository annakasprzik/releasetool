Presentations

A prototype of the tool has been presented under the title "Towards Semantic Quality Control of Automatic Subject Indexing" at TPDL-2017, Thessaloniki, Greece, September 18-21, 2017 (Martin Toepfer & Christin Seifert, 2017). That predecessor used semantic web technology as a database backend. By contrast to the prototype, the new releasetool has been reimplemented and revised substantially in several aspects. Semantic web technology is now only accessed as a service to access the thesaurus. The reimplementation has been performed in order to put more emphasize on business processes and system integration.

Automatic Subject Indexing

The releasetool does not depend on a specific subject indexing method. However, the explanation functionality of the tool is particularly of interest when fusion approaches are applied.
